Degenerative Disc Diagnosis



About this project

Artificial Intelligence has been of great benefit to many in the Medical field. One such area of interest has been diagnosing and recognizing potential medical health issues. This has been accomplished using a variety of metrics, such as X-rays, blood measurements, and other medical tests.

One area of the medical fields where Artificial Intellgence has not been fully utilized is in the chiropractic field. As such, this project is an attempt to introduce Artificial Intellgence to the chiropractic field.

Using data provided by Realigned Chiropractic as well as Artificial Intellgence, a model has been created to predict Degenerative Disc Disease. A patient can enter their non-identifiable medical metrics to be passed through the AI model, to which a likelihood of Degenerative Disc Disease will be given.
